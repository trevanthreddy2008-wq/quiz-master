Privacy Policy for Quiz Master

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This policy explains what information Quiz Master ("the app") collects and how it's used. The short version: almost everything stays on your device. Nothing is sold to anyone.

Account information

When you create an account, the email and password you choose are stored only on your own device, in the app's private local storage. They are never sent to any server we operate — there isn't one. We never see this information.

Advertising

This app shows ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device and advertising identifiers, approximate location, and other data to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's Partner Sites policy and Google's Privacy Policy. We don't control what AdMob collects beyond the settings we configure; Google is responsible for how that data is handled.

Redeem requests

If you choose to request a coin redemption, you're asked for a contact method (email or phone/WhatsApp number) so the app developer can reach you directly with a code. That information is sent via your own email or WhatsApp app straight to the developer — it does not pass through or get stored on any server we operate.

Children

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data sharing

We do not sell your information. The only third party involved is Google AdMob, for the advertising purposes described above.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change too.
